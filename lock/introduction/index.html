Before Starting#

Options for Spaced Repetition#

caution

The Spaced Repetition system can be configured with two options:

  • Option (i) : Direct consolidation upon creation or concept by concept upon validation.

  • Option (ii) : All contents are available for Spaced Repetition or a specific pool of content only.
